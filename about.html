Bruno Mars

Soul Pop Prince
Bruno Mars, born Peter Gene Hernandez, is a multi-talented American singer, songwriter, and producer known for his retro showmanship and genre-blending music

He gained fame with hits like "Just the Way You Are" and "Uptown Funk," and has won multiple Grammy Awards.

Raised in Honolulu, Hawaii, he started performing as a child and later formed the superduo Silk Sonic with Anderson Paak.

Stevie Wonder

8th wonder of the world
Stevie Wonder, born Stevland Hardaway Judkins, is an iconic American singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist.

Blind since infancy, he became a child prodigy and signed with Motown Records at the age of 11.

Known for hits like "Superstition" and "I Just Called to Say I Love You," he has won numerous Grammy Awards and influenced generations of musicians.

Aretha Franklin

Queen of soul pop
Aretha Franklin, known as the "Queen of Soul," was an iconic American singer, songwriter, and pianist.

She gained fame with timeless hits like "Respect" and "(You Make Me Feel Like) A Natural Woman."

With 18 Grammy Awards and a Presidential Medal of Freedom, she remains a symbol of musical excellence and cultural impact.
